Author/Programmer: Taylor Olson
Design Help: Duc Vu, Kaleb Lusek, Kaushik Mahida.

To open the design, navigate to the file named logInTemplate. Right click this file and choose 'open with' and open with the web browser of your choosing.

Once open, you will see a login screen. The credentials are arbitrary and once you press log-in you will be able to navigate through the design prototype.
